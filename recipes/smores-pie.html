S'mores Ice Cream Pie Recipe

S'mores Ice Cream Pie

Bring camping to your family get together or to your date with Netflix. How can you have s'more of nothing? With this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups finely crushed graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 (28 ounce) package chocolate ice cream, slightly softened
  • 2 cups miniature marshmallows, or enough to cover the top
